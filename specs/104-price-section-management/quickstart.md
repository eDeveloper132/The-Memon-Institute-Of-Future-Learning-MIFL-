# Quickstart: Price Section Management

## Prerequisites
- Administrative access to the system.
- Knowledge of PKR currency formatting.

## Setup
1. Navigate to **Admin Dashboard**.
2. Go to **Courses** or **Classes** management page.

## Key Actions
### Registering a New Course with Price
1. Click **Register New Course**.
2. Fill in the Title, Code, Credits, Department, and Teacher.
3. Locate the new **Enrollment Fee (PKR)** field.
4. Enter the amount (e.g., 5000).
5. Click **Save Course**.

### Creating a New Class with Price
1. Click **New Academic Class**.
2. Fill in the Name, Grade, Section, and Academic Year.
3. Locate the new **Enrollment Fee (PKR)** field.
4. Enter the amount.
5. Click **Create Class**.

### Updating the Price
- Use the **Edit** button on the card to update the fee within the modal.
- OR click on the **PKR amount** on the card to update it quickly via a prompt.
