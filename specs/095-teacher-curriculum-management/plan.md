## Summary
Transform the existing "Materials" page into a comprehensive Curriculum Management system. Teachers will be able to manage structured outlines and modules for their courses and classes, while admins will have the ability to lock these definitions for consistency.

## Technical Context

**Language/Version**: TypeScript / Node.js 20+
**Primary Dependencies**: Mongoose, Express
**Storage**: MongoDB
**Testing**: Manual E2E verification
