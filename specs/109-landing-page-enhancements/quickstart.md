# Quickstart: Landing Page Enhancements

## How to Verify

1. **Reveal on Scroll**:
   - Refresh the page and scroll down slowly.
   - Observe that sections (About Us, Statistics, Programs) fade in or slide up as they enter the viewport.

2. **Hero Background**:
   - Check the Hero section for new background elements (e.g., animated blobs, mesh gradients, or patterns) that add visual interest.

3. **Hover Effects**:
   - Hover over the "Get Started" and "Explore Programs" buttons.
   - Hover over the "Featured Programs" cards.
   - Verify that there are smooth transitions (scale, shadow, or color change).

4. **Icons**:
   - Check the "Our Mission" list and "Featured Programs" cards for consistent and meaningful icons.

## Performance Check
- Ensure there is no stuttering or lag during scrolling.
- Run a Lighthouse audit to confirm no layout shifts are caused by the animations.
